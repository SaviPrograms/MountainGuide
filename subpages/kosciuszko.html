Mount Kosciuszko

Continent: Australia
Country: Australia
Height: 2228 m

Mount Kosciuszko is the highest mountain in Australia.
It is located in the Snowy Mountains of New South Wales.
The mountain is a popular destination for hikers and tourists,
and there are several trails that lead to the summit.
The area around the mountain is also popular for skiing and snowboarding.
